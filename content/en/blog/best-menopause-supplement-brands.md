## Why choose a food supplement during menopause

Menopause affects approximately 14 million women in France, with first symptoms appearing around age 51 according to the French Institute for Demographic Studies. Hot flashes, sleep disturbances, mood swings, weight gain, skin dryness : 80% of menopausal women report at least one bothersome symptom, and 25% severe symptoms according to a 2024 IFOP survey. Faced with these discomforts, the **menopause food supplements** market grows by 9% annually since 2020 in France.

Choosing among the **best menopause supplement brands** requires comparing several rarely combined criteria : natural composition, organic certifications, dosage transparency, clinical studies, daily-friendly format and price. The French market offers about fifty brands, with very different positionings : classical phytotherapy, micronutrition, gemmotherapy, vitamin supplementation. The ideal **menopause food supplement** doesn't exist in absolute terms, but some brands consistently rank top in **menopause brand reviews** from professionals and users.

### Selection criteria for a good menopause supplement

Before comparing brands, here are the seven criteria selected to objectively evaluate the **menopause supplements** available on the French market in 2026 :

- Composition : plants used, dosages, presence of controversial excipients
- Certifications : organic (AB, Ecocert, Cosmos), GMP, Made in France or Europe
- Monthly cure price at recommended dosage
- Galenic format : capsules, drops, tablets, ampoules
- Specific expertise : generalist brand or specialised in women's wellness
- Clinical studies : availability of scientific publications on the formula
- Satisfaction guarantee, raw material traceability

The most studied plants for menopause remain black cohosh (Cimicifuga racemosa), red clover (Trifolium pratense), soy isoflavones, common sage, wild yam and hop. On the micronutrition side, vitamin D, magnesium, calcium and omega 3 complete classic **menopause vitamins**.

## How to properly choose your menopause food supplement

Beyond the brand, several reflexes help select a product suited to one's personal situation and optimise the chances of results.

### Essential decision criteria

The first reflex is to check the complete composition on the label, in particular the possible presence of gluten, soy, lactose, or specific allergens. Dosage transparency remains a quality marker : a brand that precisely indicates the amount of dry extract or active ingredient per capsule offers more guarantees than a product with a simple "plant extract" mention.

The galenic format influences effectiveness and adherence : drops (HerbalGem, Nutergia drinkable) guarantee rapid absorption and flexible dosage, while capsules (Arkopharma, Naturactive, Solgar) facilitate intake on the go but require optimal digestive transit. For women with frequent digestive issues (frequent during menopause), the liquid form is preferable.

The recommended cure duration is between 3 and 6 months minimum to observe tangible results. **Menopause vitamin supplements** are not miracle treatments : their effectiveness rests on regular intake, ideally combined with a balanced diet, regular physical activity and stress management.

### Mistakes to avoid

1. Combining several brands of **menopause supplements France** simultaneously without follow-up : risk of interactions and isoflavone overdose
2. Ignoring hormonal contraindications : history of breast cancer, fibroids, endometriosis require medical advice before intake
3. Stopping the cure at first signs of improvement : hormonal stabilisation requires at least 3 months of regularity
4. Prioritising the lowest price without checking composition : extreme promotions often hide insufficient dosages or controversial excipients
5. Ignoring raw material geographic origin : European gemmotherapy buds or phytotherapy plants guarantee superior traceability to Asian or American products
